Description

Immerse yourself as Valentina, a cosmonaut aboard a ship in the company of Konstantin, the AI that oversees and takes care of you while doing daily tasks for your mission. But as the days go by, your perception of reality can be affected. Will you embrace it? Break free? Or find an answer somewhere else?

History

From the creators of Finding Monsters Adventure and Rock & Rails, ANGEST is a story-driven game where your actions can affect the narrative, making each experience unique. A surreal journey presented by Black River Studios to play in the Gear VR. Welcome home, Valentina.

Features

  • Strong storytelling experience, based on the sense of introspection;
  • Piece together a story that unfolds as you observe and explore;
  • Branching storyline based on players decisions during tasks and events that makes each experience unique;
  • A mysterious environment that expands as you explore;
